Eligibility Criteria

Age Limit

General: 18–23/25 years (Constable). Up to 35 for specific officer ranks. Relaxations: OBC (3y), SC/ST (5y).

Education

10th/12th for Constable. Degree for Sub-Inspector and Assistant Commandant roles.

Citizenship

Must be a citizen of India. Specific Nepal/Bhutan provisions apply for certain CAPF posts.

Documents

Keep Aadhar, Domicile, Caste certificate, and all marksheets ready for verification rounds.

Physical Standards (PET)

Height

Male: 170cm

Female: 157cm

Chest (Male)

80 - 85 cm

Min 5cm expansion

Written Strategy

  • Prioritize Reasoning, GA, Maths, and Hindi/English.
  • Solve one Previous Year Question Paper daily.
  • Follow a consistent 6-8 hour rigorous study routine.
