Eligibility Criteria

Age Limit

General candidates: 18–23/25 years (Constable) and up to 35 for specific officer ranks. Age relaxation for OBC (3y) and SC/ST (5y).

Education

10th/12th pass for Constable roles. Graduation required for Sub-Inspector and Assistant Commandant positions.

Citizenship

Must be a citizen of India. Some posts in CAPF allow candidates from Nepal and Bhutan as per Govt regulations.

Required Documents

Aadhar card, Domicile certificate, Caste certificate (if applicable), and all marksheets ready for verification.

Physical Standards (PST/PET)

Female: 157 cm

Written Exam Strategy

  • Focus on Reasoning, General Awareness, Maths, and Hindi/English.
  • Practice previous year question papers daily.
  • Maintain a 6-hour consistent study schedule.

Morning Routine

  • 04:30 AM — Wake up and Hydrate
  • 05:00 AM — 5km Run & Stretching
  • 07:00 AM — High Protein Breakfast (Sprouts/Eggs)
